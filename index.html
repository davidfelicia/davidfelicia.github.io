Directions to Venue
  • By Car
  • By MRT or Bus

If you are driving, you can reach The Chapel @ Imaginarium via
- Exiting PIE via exit 11
- Turn into Sims Ave
- Turn into Eunos Road 2
- Turn into Eunos Ave 3
- Look for the sign 115 Eunos Ave 3

Parking is available within the building @ $1.20 per hour. If the built-up lots are full, you can park along the roads inside the building, as long as you don't obstruct traffic. Coupon parking lots can be found along Eunos Ave 3 and Eunos Road 5.

Sorry ah, they don't offer complimentary parking :(

The easiest way to get to The Chapel @ Imaginarium is via MRT
- Alight at Paya Lebar MRT Station
- Exit towards Singpost Building
- Walk along the park connector beneath MRT tracks
- Look for the sign 115 Eunos Ave 3 at your left

If you intend to travel by Bus, the nearest bus stops are at Sims Ave, where you will need to walk through the HDB and MRT tracks to reach The Chapel @ Imaginarium:
- Buses at Blk 414: 7, 26, 28, 30
- Buses at Blk 416: 2, 13, 21, 24, 51, 67, 76, 154, 155
